Trained as a student of the “The Tech Academy,” I’m an aspiring Web & Software Developer eager to take on new ventures and projects in the world of coding.

I’m a 26-year-old Filipino American based in Las Vegas Nevada. Born and raised in both the PDX and Bay Area, I’ve been fortunate to take on many ventures into different environments that have shaped me into who I am today.

When I’m not coding, I spend most of my time creating music and playing games. I’ve been an aspiring musician since the age of 10, as well as an avid gamer as a result of being born into a family with careers in the gaming scene.
Coding has been an interest of mine since the mid-2000s, specifically when the popular social networking site “Myspace” was around. Through there, I was given a small introduction to web development by working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript at an early age.

As both a former student in Psychology and a former coffee shop Barista and General Manager, I believed it was time to move forward onto my venture. This led to my transition into the development scene, where I had moved from my college town in Southern California back to my childhood home of Portland, OR to pursue this field as a student of “The Tech Academy.”
Expecting to graduate in the middle of March, I look forward to branching out into the development scene to take on any new ventures and projects.
